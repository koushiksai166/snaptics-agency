DVPL Solutions Ltd

Innovative web application redesign and SEO optimization to drive your business forward.

Project Overview

Client

DVPL Solutions Ltd

Services

UX Research, Wireframing, UI Design

Duration

April 23, 2024 - Present

Challenges

  • The website required key improvements across multiple areas to enhance performance and growth.
  • Technical SEO enhancements were needed to increase organic visibility.
  • A steady flow of high-quality content was essential to maintain user engagement.
  • The user experience and site navigation required refinement to improve retention.
  • Lead conversion was hindered by insufficient calls-to-action and forms.
  • The analytics setup lacked depth, limiting the ability to measure performance and optimize ROI.

Solutions

  • Conducted a full technical SEO audit and improved metadata, site speed, and mobile responsiveness.
  • Launched a blog and knowledge hub focusing on AI use cases, analytics trends, and enterprise applications.
  • Streamlined website structure and CTAs to support lead conversion and redesigned navigation.
  • Integrated lead magnets like downloadable assets, contact forms, and smart popups.
  • Implemented tools like Google Analytics 4, Hotjar, and Tag Manager for comprehensive tracking.
  • Introduced 'Akademia' as a branded program for recruitment, training, and partner onboarding.
